V4.0.3 - Corrective Release
===========================

This release fixes V4.0.2 usability issues and reorganises the app around module selection.

Highlights:
- First-open module dashboard.
- Height Equipment, Vehicle Checks and Ops Management separated.
- Certificates UI fixed.
- Users/Admin access preserved within Height Equipment.
- Vehicle Checks simplified to one Vehicle Inspection Checklist.
- Checklist answers start blank and are required.
- Photo upload only on exterior vehicle cleaning item.
- Cab and tray cleaning combined.
- Preventive maintenance standard templates introduced.
- New water blaster/engine/pump equipment can auto-receive standard schedules.

Known later work:
- Write fuller maintenance guides for each procedure after exact models/manuals are confirmed.
- Build Employee Portal, central task list, safety reports and chemical inventory in a later phase.
