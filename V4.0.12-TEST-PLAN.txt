V4.0.12 Test Plan

1. App load
- Open https://sprayandwash.github.io/height-safety-register/?v=4.0.12
- Hard refresh with Ctrl + F5.
- Confirm the header shows Version 4.0.12.

2. Height Equipment layout
- Open Height Equipment from Home.
- Confirm Home button appears above the Height Equipment title.
- Confirm the tab row contains Dashboard, Equipment, New Inspection, Due / Failed, Reports, Certificates and Qualifications only.
- Confirm Home is not mixed into the Height tab row.
- Confirm Users and Admin tabs are not visible.

3. Admin permissions
- Open Admin.
- Confirm there is no permission preset dropdown.
- Confirm there is no role preset guide.
- Confirm pre-loaded user form uses role checkboxes.
- Confirm signed-in users use role checkboxes.
- Change a test user's roles, save, refresh and confirm roles persist.

4. Regression checks
- Open Vehicle Checks and confirm the vehicle checklist still loads.
- Open Ops Management and confirm Dashboard, Assets, Inspection History, Tasks, Preventive Maintenance and Guides still load.
- Open Height Certificates and confirm selected item certificate generation still works.
- Confirm the app logo still returns to Home.
