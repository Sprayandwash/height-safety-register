V4.0.3 Test Plan
================

After install:
1. Open app with ?v=4.0.3 and hard refresh.
2. Confirm the app opens to the module dashboard, not directly to Height Safety tabs.
3. Confirm Admin/Brendan can see:
   - Height Equipment
   - Vehicle Checks
   - Ops Management
4. Confirm a normal tool/Inspector user can see only:
   - Height Equipment
   - Vehicle Checks
   and not Ops Management.
5. Open Height Equipment.
   - Confirm Dashboard, Equipment, New Inspection, Due / Failed, Reports, Certificates, Users/Admin tabs appear according to role.
6. Open Certificates.
   - Confirm certificate batch type dropdown works.
   - Confirm equipment type dropdown populates.
   - Confirm selected item list populates.
7. Open Users as Admin.
   - Confirm user list and role checkboxes appear.
8. Return to module dashboard.
9. Open Vehicle Checks.
   - Confirm only Vehicle Inspection Checklist is shown.
   - Confirm there is no inspection template dropdown.
   - Confirm inspector name is prefilled from user/profile and read-only.
   - Confirm every select starts as No response.
   - Try submitting without answering all items; app should block submission.
   - Confirm photo upload appears only under the exterior vehicle wash item.
   - Confirm cab/tray cleaning is one combined item.
   - Submit a test vehicle check with one Issue to report.
   - Confirm a maintenance task is created.
10. Open Ops Management.
   - Confirm registers, history, maintenance, preventive maintenance and guides are available.
   - Confirm Vehicle Checks are not duplicated here as a staff checklist workflow.
11. Preventive Maintenance:
   - Confirm standard template area appears.
   - Apply templates to one water blaster item.
   - Apply templates to all equipment of same type.
   - Confirm schedules are created/updated.
12. Add a new washing equipment item with type Water Blaster.
   - Confirm standard preventive schedules are automatically created.
13. Confirm existing Height Safety equipment records still load and inspections still work.
